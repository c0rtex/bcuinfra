Next Steps

What you just did:

You completed the Application for Help With Medicare Prescription Drug Plan Costs.

What we will do:

We will process your application as quickly as possible. We will contact you if we need more information. When we finish, we will send you a letter to let you know if you qualify for extra help. We will also send you information about the Medicare Prescription Drug program and tell you what you should do next.

What you need to do:

Carefully read the letter we send you. The information we provide will tell you what you should do next. Please remember if you qualify for this extra help, you must enroll in a Medicare prescription drug plan.

If you do not choose a Medicare prescription drug plan, Medicare will choose one for you to be sure you get this benefit. If you wait for Medicare to enroll you, you may have months for which you do not have the prescription drug coverage. For information about prescription drug plans in your area, you may call toll-free 1-800-MEDICARE (1-800-633-4227) or visit www.medicare.gov. If you are deaf or hard of hearing, you may call the Medicare TTY number toll-free at 1-877-486-2048.
