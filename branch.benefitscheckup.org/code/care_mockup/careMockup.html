How to Prepare

Gather the information you'll need

  • Zip code
  • Date of birth
  • Length of time in current residence
  • Current income, assets, and expenses
  • Names of prescription medications

What to Expect

  • Fill out the Questionnaire – it typically takes about 20 minutes.
  • Receive a report that shows you how you could save money on health care, prescription drugs, meals, utilities, taxes, and more.
  • The report includes information about Medicare, Medicaid, and many other public and private programs.
