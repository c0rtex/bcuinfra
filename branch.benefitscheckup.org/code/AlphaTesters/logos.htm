Some Sample Logos

These logos are provided to help you in setting-up your organizational profile within the CSO version of BenefitsCheckUp. They are already properly sized. Sizing logos isn't a difficult task, but requires specialized software not always readily available. If you want to use any of these logos with your temporary organization profile, please do so.

A few quick tips:
  • Identify the temporary logo that you'd like to use (please note: you can change the logo you use within your organizational profile whenever you'd like)
  • "Right Click" on the logo you'd like to use
  • View the Pop-Up menu and find "Save Image As" (In Netscape Browsers) or "Save Picture As" (in Microsoft's Internet Explorers)
  • Select to save the image
  • Identify an easy to find location on your local computer ..."Desktop" often is easiest
  • Access "Update Profile" off the CSO Main Menu (Home) of the CSO version of BenefitsCheckUp and when there select to "Change Logo" (located at the bottom of the screen), you'll be presented with a window asking to identify what logo you'd like to use, click on browse and select the logo you placed on your desktop or where you placed the temporary logo. Finally, select "Update Logo" and you'll be returned to the Organizational Profile page with your new logo in place.
