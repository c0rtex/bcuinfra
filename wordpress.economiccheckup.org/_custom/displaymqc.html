Basics

Medicare QuickCheck is a brief, confidential questionnaire that looks at your current coverage and finds out if you may be able to save money on your Medicare costs. At the end of the QuickCheck, you’ll receive a free, personalized report that outlines your options and recommendations. You’ll also get advice on how to connect with someone who can guide you through enrollment or changing plans. you meet them. When you are done you will get a customized report with recommendations for community services that can help you make the most of your money. It will take approximately 30 minutes to complete this questionnaire.

Getting Started

Information You Will Need For an MedicareQuickCheck

  • Proof of age and residency for self and spouse
  • Most recent year’s tax returns (if applicable)
  • Chronic health conditions and disability status of self and spouse
  • Health insurance status for self and spouse
  • Homeowner’s insurance status and amount owed on home (if applicable)
  • The following worksheet ( downloadable below) filled out with all applicable information
