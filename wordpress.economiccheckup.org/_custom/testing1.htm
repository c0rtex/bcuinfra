Recommended Actions

Based on your responses, we recommend that you:


  1. Take action before December 7, 2013 when Medicare's annual Open Enrollment season ends. Get help with comparing and evaluating plan using one or more of these options:
    • "Do-it-yourself" by using Medicare.gov, the federal government's official website. Learn More.
      • Medicare.gov, the official U.S. government website, has comprehensive information about Medicare and supplemental insurance.
      • The Medicare Plan Finder is an online tool that enables you to compare prescription drug and Medicare Advantage plans. You can compare up to three plans at one time.
      • Call 1-800-MEDICARE (1-800-633-4227) to get help with Medicare questions. TTY users should call 1-877-486-2048.
    • Get help from a volunteer or paid staff member through your State Health Insurance Counseling program. Learn More.
      • SHIP volunteers and staff provide free, objective assistance to people with Medicare and their families. Because Open Enrollment season is a very busy time, you may have to wait to get an appointment.
      • To contact your state SHIP office:

        New York Health Insurance Information Counseling and Assistance Program (HIICAP)

        Toll-Free: (800) 342-9871
        Phone: 311 (within NYC)
        Spanish Line: (800) 342-9871 (press 2 for Spanish)
        Translation Service: (800) 342-9871 (press 3 for other languages)
        Web address: http://www.aging.ny.gov/
        Hours: 8:30 a.m. – 4:30 p.m. (Monday – Friday)
        More info: If you leave a message for a counselor, you should get a call back within 24 to 48 hours.
