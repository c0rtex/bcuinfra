Living on a fixed income? Find food programs that can help.

Join the 1 million people who have found over $1.5 billion in food benefits.

Millions of older Americans use the Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program (SNAP) and their local food bank to put healthy food on the table. The National Council on Aging and Feeding America are teaming up to connect older Americans with these food programs in their community.

"Thanks to SNAP, I have an extra $108 every month to help pay for food."
