NCOA is building upon the platform of BenefitsCheckUp to develop and test an innovative decision support tool that will help millions of low and middle income seniors who are facing economic difficulties. The EconomicCheckUp, currently available as a prototype, is an online service to help seniors and the agencies that work with them:
       
  assess their financial needs;  
       
  find a wide array of community counseling and assistance services that might be appropriate to help them meet their needs; and
       
  provide hands-on assistance in accessing these services.  
       
       
Thank you for helping us pilot test the EconomicCheckUp. With your help, we look forward to refining the tool to better meet the needs of older adults experiencing economic distress.
