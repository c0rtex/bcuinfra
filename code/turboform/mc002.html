Important Information

You might have received a notice from Social Security advising you of an earlier time period for filing your application. If you did, it was because you or someone you know contacted us on your behalf about filing an application for the extra help before you started this Internet application.

Generally, it is a good idea to file within that earlier period because if you are approved for the extra help, your approval will be retroactive to the first day of the month in which you first filed an application. (For example, if you filed your application in May and received your approval letter in July, you would be eligible for the extra help as of May 1.) This is important because if you are already in a Medicare drug plan and paying monthly premiums, you can be reimbursed for those premiums.
