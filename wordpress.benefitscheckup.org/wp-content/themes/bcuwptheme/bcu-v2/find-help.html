Home  >  Find Help

BenefitsCheckUp helps you find state, federal, and private benefits programs available where you live. You may find a full range of benefits that can help pay for prescriptions, health care, food, utilities, and more. Also help with tax relief, transportation, legal issues, or finding work. About Us.

Paying for Medicine

Medicare Rx Extra Help

If you have Medicare and have limited income and resources, you may be able to get extra help paying for your prescriptions.

Paying for Food

Food Programs in Your State

Get the food you need to stay healthy. Find program information, application forms and link to other resources in your state.

Prescription Assistance Programs

Find programs that can help you save money on prescriptions and other health care costs.

Screen for Food Programs

Find out which food programs you may qualify for in your state.

More Places to Get Help

The National Council on Aging has other websites you may want to visit. Learn how to stay healthy, get your Medicare questions answered, afford to stay in your home, and more. Visit NOCA.org
